About Me

"Every great dream, begins with a dreamer. Always remember, you have within you the strength, the patience, and the passion to reach for the stars to change the world.” -Harriet Tubman

I remember doing a project in middle school on Harriet Tubman, the woman who lead the underground railroad, and coming across this quote. It has stuck with me ever since. I have had the passion for creativity: reading, writing, drawing, coding even, for the past thirteen years. I have expanded on it in ways I never knew possible, ways I didn’t dream of when I was a kid. But I am grateful, every day, that I have stuck with it close to my heart.

I am an aspiring full-stacked developer who has worked with a pleathora of programs over the years to create web and graphic designs while also learning about web development. As passionate as I am about art work from drawing and writing, web design and developing seems to be an excellent way to bring it all together. I can only hope to expand on this, practicing and learning to keep things simple and inspiring others in the process.

There was a lot to learn, and there will be so much more to grasp later on. It has driven me to things I never knew about and hope that I will continue to learn all I can. I continue to aspire to be a full-stacked developer – knowledge of front and back end developing will be useful to everything I want to do. I hope with this knowledge, I will be able to join a team and build projects for others who may need them but not have the skills to do so.

My passion and pride drive creativeness, while also keeping cleanliness, modern and unique approaches to design will make your site one that people enjoy coming back to.
